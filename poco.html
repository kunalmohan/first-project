Product Description

Meet the POCO F1 - the first flagship smartphone from POCO by Xiaomi. The POCO F1 sports Qualcomm flagship Snapdragon 845 processor, an octa-core CPU with a maximum clock speed of 2.8 GHz which is supported by 8 GB of LPDDR4X RAM. It is coupled with a LiquidCool Technology that allows the device to sustain peak performance for a longer period of time. On the back, it features a 12MP + 5MP Dual Pixel AI dual camera setup. The main camera sensor features 1.4 um pixels, Dual Pixel Autofocus, and Multi-frame noise reduction. On the front, it sports a 20 MP high-res front camera and IR Face unlock. POCO F1 also boasts of a massive 4000 mAh (typ) battery with Quick Charge 3.0 to keep you going all-day long.

Faster, lighter, and smoother than ever, the POCO F1 aims to help you achieve new levels of greatness regardless of whether you're gaming, working, or watching videos. It is for a reason that this new Xiaomi addition is labelled the ‘Master of Speed’.

Performance - Beat This!

Enjoy an unbeatable performance on a low power consumption, courtesy the phone’s Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 mobile platform. You get Snapdragon’s powerful AI engine on an advanced flagship 10nm class chip – work, game, or multi-task, everything is seamless.

LiquidCool Technology

Normally reserved for hardcore gaming smartphones, this technology keeps the POCO F1’s CPU cool. Its Snapdragon 845 processor maintains its peak performance and boasts a high frequency output no matter how intensely you game.

Full-day Battery Charge

Juice up your phone quickly; it supports the Quick Charge 3.0 system which replenishes the battery’s power in no time. Once fully charged, its 4000 mAh lithium polymer battery powers up to 30 hours of calling and up to 146 hours of audio playback.

Picture-perfect Shots

The 12 MP + 5 MP dual rear camera system features dual pixel autofocus and a large 1.4 micrometer pixel sensor in the primary lens. These come together to deliver pictures with enhanced brightness, clarity, and quality. This camera’s autofocus speed along with EIS (Electronic Image Stabilisation) and Hardware multi-frame noise reduction is commendable, especially while clicking pictures which are in motion or in low-light conditions.

AI Scene Detection

The AI cameras intelligently distinguish across 206 scenes and automatically adjust the exposure, saturation in media files, and make other photo optimizations. The POCO F1 packs an additional Scene Detection feature that's designed specially for the Indian smartphone user.

High-res Front Camera

The 20 MP high-res sensor coupled with the 4-in-1 Super Pixel technology (2 micrometer-large pixel) in the front camera gives you selfies which are as good as they can get. It’s time to revamp your Instagram page with studio-like shots; thanks to the AI Beautify 4.0 and the Bokeh Effect feature.

Lighter, Faster, and Smoother with MIUI

Tailor-made for POCO, MIUI (user interface) equips this phone with a turbocharged engine to give you a faster performance, more sensitive swipe movements, and a lot more such benefits. The customizable App Drawer for POCO Launcher intelligently categorises your apps so searching for them is simple and quick. MIUI for POCO is also tasked with the work of securing your phone with the latest patches and updates from Google.

IR Face Unlock

Access your phone’s contents without touching it. Infrared light on the device’s camera identifies your face and unlocks the phone even in the dark.

If Looks Could Kill

The POCO F1 uses the same Kevlar armed fibre which is used for applications in aviation, military, and high-end cycling. This design detailing gives the phone a sophisticated texture and ensures you a firm grip on it, whilst also protecting the device from everyday wear and tear.

One Phone, Two Ports

Other than the required type-C charging port, this phone features a 3.5 mm audio port.

A Full Screen Display

Enjoy an intuitive and immersive viewing experience on its 18.7:9 edge-to-edge display. Protected by the renowned Corning Gorilla Glass, its 15.69 cm (6.18) Full-HD+ screen is built to last.

An Expansive Internal Storage

Now store as many songs and photos on your phone as you want. This smartphone comes with up to 64 GB of ROM.
